Goals

...miles we have to go.




Major Goal

To inculcate a culture of robotics and automation in GCOEA to solve real-world problems. Also, to help students achieve credible skill in designing and developing robust autonomous machines for the future and create an environment of research and development in robotics in the next 5 years. To perform pioneering work in mechanical and systems design and serve as a link between the students and industry which will help their professional careers.




Future Prospects

The Forum is engaged in finding sponsors from industry so as to solve the issues of funding and technical in-experience at the same time. The forum also looks to provide direct industry linking to provide internships and industry-oriented projects to its members.

The Forum is looking forward to get connected with the technical forums of other elite institutions across India to expand our boundaries of technical knowledge and experience. To connect with our team, click here

Any personnel from industry or Alumni of GCOE, Amravati interested in getting associated with the forum is welcome and can contact the Student Coordinator directly.
